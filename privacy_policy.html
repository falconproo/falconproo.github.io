Privacy Policy – Falcon Pro App

Last updated: January 2025

Falcon Pro App (“we”, “our”, or “us”) is a Facebook-integrated application used for publishing posts to a Facebook Page selected by the user. This application does not collect, store, or process any personal data outside Facebook’s platform.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

Falcon Pro App does not collect any of the following:

  • Names or personal identities
  • Email addresses
  • Phone numbers
  • Passwords
  • Location data
  • Payment information
  • Browser cookies or tracking data

2. Facebook Permissions

The app may request Facebook permissions solely for the purpose of publishing content on a Facebook Page chosen by the user. Permissions are granted through Facebook’s official OAuth login system. We do not store or access your Facebook login details.

3. Use of Access Tokens

Access tokens generated by Facebook are used only to allow the app to publish posts on your selected Page. We do not store, share, or save any access tokens outside Facebook’s secure environment. All token handling follows Facebook’s platform policies.

4. No External Data Storage

We do not store or save any data on external servers. All operations happen directly on Facebook’s servers through their API.

5. No Data Sharing

We do not share any user data with third parties because no user data is collected in the first place.

6. External Links

The app may include links to Facebook or GitHub for documentation or integration. These external sites have their own privacy policies.

7. Changes to the Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy if required by Facebook’s platform rules. Updated versions will always be available on this page.

8. Contact

For any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please reach out through the official Falcon Pro Facebook Page.
